GPT, or Generative Pre-trained Transformer, is a revolutionary artificial intelligence model that has significantly advanced the field of natural language processing. Developed by OpenAI, GPT is a deep learning model based on the Transformer architecture. It has been designed to understand and generate human-like text, making it a versatile tool for a wide range of applications.


GPT is "pre-trained" in the sense that it is initially exposed to vast amounts of text data from the internet, allowing it to learn the patterns, grammar, and context of human language. This pre-training phase equips GPT with a strong foundation for understanding and generating text in a coherent and contextually relevant manner.
