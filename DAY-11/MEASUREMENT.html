Learning measurements in web-development

CSS-Units


Absolute (fixed) units.


  • cm - centimeters
  • mm - millimeters
  • in - inches
  • px - pixels
  • pt - points
  • pc - picas

relative (fluid) units.


  • em - Rel to container
  • ex - Rel to height
  • ch - Rel to the width of 0
  • rem - Rel to font-size of root element
  • vw - Rel to 1% of viewpoint width
  • vh - Rel to 1% of viewpoint height
  • vmin - rel to 1% vieport smaller dimension
  • vmax - Rel to 1% of viewport larger dimension
  • % - Rel to parent element
